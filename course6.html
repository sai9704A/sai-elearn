Course Overview

Learn the fundamentals of graphic design, including principles of design, typography, color theory, and how to use industry-standard tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Course Content

Module 1: Introduction to Graphic Design

Understand the basic principles of design such as balance, contrast, alignment, and repetition.

Module 2: Typography

Learn the art of choosing and using typefaces, font styles, and creating legible and attractive typography.

Module 3: Color Theory

Study how colors work together and how to apply color theory to enhance your design projects.

Module 4: Graphic Design Tools

Get hands-on experience with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign for creating professional designs.
