Course Overview

This course is designed to equip you with essential business management skills, including strategic planning, leadership, communication, and decision-making to effectively manage teams and projects.

Course Content

Module 1: Introduction to Business Management

Understand the fundamentals of business management, including organizational structures, management functions, and the role of a manager in driving success.

Module 2: Strategic Planning

Learn how to create long-term business strategies, analyze market trends, and plan for growth and sustainability in a competitive environment.

Module 3: Leadership and Team Management

Explore leadership theories, team dynamics, and best practices for motivating and managing diverse teams to achieve company goals.

Module 4: Communication and Decision Making

Master the art of clear and effective communication, and understand decision-making models and frameworks to improve business outcomes.
