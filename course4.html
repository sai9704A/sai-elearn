Course Overview

This course covers the fundamentals of digital marketing strategies, including SEO, social media marketing, content marketing, and paid advertising campaigns.

Course Content

Module 1: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Understand the key concepts of digital marketing and the various platforms for reaching your audience.

Module 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Learn the best practices for improving your website's visibility on search engines like Google.

Module 3: Social Media Marketing

Develop strategies to promote your brand and engage your audience on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Module 4: Paid Advertising

Master the use of paid advertising platforms like Google Ads and Facebook Ads to increase website traffic.
